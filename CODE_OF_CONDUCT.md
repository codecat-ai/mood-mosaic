# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Mood Mosaic respectful and constructive for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or location.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Focus criticism on code, documentation, and project outcomes.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence and tests.
- Respect privacy and avoid requesting personal mood or health details from
  contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic demands or bad-faith disruption.
- Medical, diagnostic, or crisis claims presented as project guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when
behavior violates this code. Reports can be made through the repository's normal
maintainer contact path.
